The primary customers of this application are health-conscious individuals who are interetsted in improving their health habits.


The application provides the user with their HealthScore and makes recommendations that will allow the user to improve it by providing information about local fitness classes.

Fitness-centers and gyms can directly benefit from this application since it promotes the user engagement with in-person fitness classes.


In addition, this app can be used by medical professionals to recommend specific activities to patients based on their needs.
